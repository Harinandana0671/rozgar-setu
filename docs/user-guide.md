## Getting Started

When you first open Rozgar Setu, you'll see the **home page** with two main options:

- **👷 Worker / Laborer** - If you're looking for work
- **🏢 Employer** - If you need workers

Simply click on your role to proceed.

---

## For Workers

### Creating Your Profile

1. Click **"Worker / Laborer"** from the home page
2. Click **"Add Profile"** from the bottom navigation
3. Fill in the form with:
   - **Full Name** - Your name
   - **Phone** - Your 10-digit mobile number
   - **Type of Work** - Select your skill (Mason, Plumber, etc.)
   - **City** - Your city
   - **Area** - Your locality/area
   - **Wage/Daily Rate** - Your expected daily wage
   - **Experience** - Years of experience
   - **Availability** - Check days you're available

4. Click **"Save Profile"** to submit

Your profile is now saved and visible to employers!

---

### Finding Work

#### Using Quick Search
1. Go to the **Home** page
2. In the "Quick Search" section, either:
   - Type a keyword in the search box (e.g., "Mason job")
   - Click on a category button (Mason, Plumber, etc.)
3. View all matching jobs in the search results

#### Using Voice Search
1. Click the 🎤 **"Search with Voice"** button
2. Speak your search query
3. The app will transcribe and search automatically

#### Browsing Available Jobs
1. Go to **Search** (bottom nav)
2. Browse the available jobs posted by employers
3. Click **"Apply"** to apply for a job
4. Your contact will be shared with the employer

---

### Managing Your Profile

1. Click **"Profile"** from the bottom navigation
2. View and edit your profile information
3. Update your availability, location, or wage as needed

---

## For Employers

### Posting a Job

1. Click **"Employer"** from the home page
2. Click **"Post Job"** from the bottom navigation
3. Fill in the job details:
   - **Job Title** - Brief description (e.g., "Need a Plumber for bathroom repair")
   - **Type of Worker** - Select the skill needed
   - **City & Area** - Where the work is located
   - **When?** - Timeline (Today, Tomorrow, This Week)
   - **Payment** - Budget (e.g., ₹500-800)
   - **Job Description** - Detailed description of the work
   - **Your Name & Phone** - Contact information

4. Click **"Post Job"** to submit

Your job is now live and workers can apply!

---

### Finding Workers

#### Searching for Workers by Skill
1. Click the **"Search"** tab
2. Use the search bar to find workers by:
   - Work type (Mason, Plumber, etc.)
   - Location
   - Availability

#### Viewing Worker Profiles
1. From search results, click on a worker card
2. View their:
   - Experience level
   - Daily wage/rate
   - Availability
   - Location
3. Contact them through their phone number

#### Filtering by Availability
- Filter for workers available **today** for urgent jobs
- Use the "Available Today" section on the home page

---

### Managing Job Posts

1. Click **"Profile"** from the bottom navigation
2. View all your posted jobs
3. See:
   - Number of applications
   - Job status
   - Posted date
   - Worker applicants

## Tips & Best Practices

### For Workers
✅ **Do:**
- Keep your profile updated
- Add a clear photo/avatar when available
- Be specific about your skills
- Respond quickly to job inquiries

❌ **Don't:**
- Share personal financial details
- Agree to payment outside the app (future)
- Post irrelevant information

### For Employers
✅ **Do:**
- Post detailed job descriptions
- Be clear about payment and timeline
- Contact workers promptly
- Review past work/ratings (future feature)

❌ **Don't:**
- Post unrealistic wages
- Spam multiple posts
- Share sensitive information

## FAQ

### Q: Is my data safe?
**A:** Currently, data is stored locally in your browser. We recommend using a reputable internet connection. Future versions will use encrypted cloud storage.

### Q: How do workers get paid?
**A:** Payment arrangement is made directly between the worker and employer. Future versions will support integrated payment gateway.

### Q: Can I delete my profile?
**A:** Yes, go to Profile → Delete Profile. This will remove all your data.

### Q: What if I'm both a worker and an employer?
**A:** You can switch between roles on the home page anytime!

### Q: Is voice search available on all devices?
**A:** Voice search works on modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). Older browsers may not support this feature.

### Q: How do I report a bad experience?
**A:** Use the "Report" button on any profile or job. A moderator will review and take action.

### Q: Can I chat with workers/employers?
**A:** Not in the current version. Contact information is shared directly. Chat feature coming soon!

### Q: What happens after I apply for a job?
**A:** Your application is submitted. The employer reviews and contacts you via the phone number provided.
